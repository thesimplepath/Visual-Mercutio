Welcome to the Utility Services Portal
This site enables web service providers to offer their services using a contract. Service requestors can contract the use of services as well as check their individual usage. Service providers can use the accounting service to generate bills for contracted service requestors.
Service Provider:
A Service Provider offers contracts which get signed by Service Requestors. This establishs a business relationship which is required before using a service.

As a Service Provider, you define contracts which comprise rating and payment models, use the metering and accounting service for accounting and billing.
Service Requestor:
As a Service Requestor you can contract to use available service.
